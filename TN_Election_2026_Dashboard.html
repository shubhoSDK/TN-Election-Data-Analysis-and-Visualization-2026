01
The New Force
TVK entered the 2026 election as a first-time contestant and won more seats than any other party — 108 of 234 — with 34.9% of total votes cast statewide. No party had achieved this in a debut since the state's formation.
02
The Great Flip
163 of 234 constituencies changed winning party. The largest single flow was 65 DMK-held seats moving to TVK. AIADMK contributed 26. The result is a fundamentally reshuffled political map across all six regions.
03
The Closer Fight
A three-way split compressed winning margins to their tightest in recent history. Just 13 seats were decided with over 50% of votes (down from 70 in 2021). 64 seats were won with under 35% — up from just 2.
01 Story One
State-wide Vote Share Shift
All votes cast across 234 constituencies · 2021 vs 2026 comparison
Key shift: DMK shed 13.5 percentage points and AIADMK shed 12.1 points between elections. TVK's 34.9% is the single largest share of any party in 2026 — exceeding even DMK's tally in 2021 by comparison.
